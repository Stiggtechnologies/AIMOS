## 8:00 AM – FIRST PATIENT ARRIVAL

### Front Desk Workflow

**8:00 - Welcome**
- Greet patient warmly
- Script: "Welcome to Alberta Injury Management South Commons. How can we help you today?"

**8:02 - Confirm Appointment**
- Verify name and time in AIM OS

- Provide digital intake form
- Collect insurance information
- **CRITICAL:** Ask "How did you hear about us?" (record referral source)

---

## 8:10 AM – FIRST CLINICAL ASSESSMENT

**Responsible:** Lead Physiotherapist
**Duration:** 30 minutes

### Initial Assessment Protocol

**1. Patient Introduction (2 min)**

Script:
> "Tell me about the issue you're experiencing and what activities are most affected."

**2. Assessment (15 min)**
- Movement analysis
- Range of motion testing
- Strength testing
- Pain assessment (VAS scale)

**3. Diagnosis Explanation (5 min)**

Explain clearly:
- What the injury is
- Why it happened
- Expected recovery timeline

**Patient must understand their condition**

**4. Treatment Plan (8 min)**

Define:
- Number of visits expected
- Treatment approach
- Exercise rehabilitation plan
- Milestones to expect

---

## 8:40 AM – FIRST TREATMENT SESSION

**Duration:** 20 minutes

### Treatment Components
1. **Manual therapy** (10 min)
   - Soft tissue work
   - Joint mobilization

2. **Exercise prescription** (10 min)
   - Corrective exercises
   - Load progression
   - Use gym floor if appropriate

**Document in AIM OS immediately after session**
