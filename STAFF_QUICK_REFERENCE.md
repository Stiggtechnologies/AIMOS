## QUICK START FOR FRONT DESK

### Logging In
1. Go to: `https://aim-os.com`
2. Enter your email: `yourname@aim.clinic`
3. Enter your password (provided by manager)
4. You'll land on the Dashboard

### Patient Check-In (60 seconds)
1. Click **Scheduler** in top menu
2. Find patient's name in today's schedule
3. Click on their appointment card
4. Click **Check In** button
5. Status changes to "Checked In" ✓
6. Clinician automatically notified

### Booking New Appointment
1. Click **Scheduler**
2. Click **+ New Appointment** (top right)
3. Select **Patient** (search or create new)
4. Select **Service** (e.g., "Physiotherapy Initial Assessment")
5. Select **Date & Time**
6. Select **Provider** (clinician)
7. Click **Book Appointment**
8. System validates (checks for conflicts)
9. Confirmation code generated
10. SMS sent automatically

### Creating New Patient
1. Click **Patients** in menu
2. Click **+ New Patient**
3. Fill in:
   - First Name *
   - Last Name *
   - Phone Number *
   - Email (optional)
   - Date of Birth
   - Address
4. Click **Save**
5. Patient now searchable in system

### Processing Payment
1. After appointment, click **Create Invoice**
2. Add line items:
   - Select service (auto-fills price)
   - Add any retail products
3. System calculates tax automatically
4. Click **Generate Invoice**
5. Click **Record Payment**
6. Select payment method:
   - Cash
   - Credit Card
   - Debit
   - Direct Billing (insurance)
   - E-transfer
7. Enter amount received
8. Click **Process Payment**
9. Print receipt

### Retail Sale (No Appointment)
1. Click **Retail** in menu
2. Click **Quick Sale**
3. Add products to cart
4. Process payment same as above
5. Inventory automatically updated

## QUICK START FOR CLINICIANS

### Viewing Your Schedule
1. Log in to AIM OS
2. Click **Scheduler**
3. Filter by **Your Name** (top left)
4. See your day's appointments
5. Color codes:
   - Blue = Scheduled
   - Yellow = Checked In (ready for you!)
   - Green = In Progress
   - Dark Green = Completed

### Starting an Appointment
1. Click on checked-in patient
2. Click **Start Session**
3. Status → "In Progress"
4. Timer starts automatically

### Clinical Charting (SOAP Note)
1. Open patient appointment
2. Click **Clinical Notes** tab
3. Fill in SOAP note:

**Subjective:**
- Chief complaint
- History of present illness
- Patient's description

**Objective:**
- Observations
- Range of motion
- Palpation findings
- Special tests

**Assessment:**
- Diagnosis/impression
- Problem list

**Plan:**
- Treatment provided today
- Home exercise program
- Follow-up schedule
- Goals

4. Click **Save Note**
5. Timestamp and your name auto-saved

### Prescribing Exercise Program
1. Click **Exercise Library** tab
2. Search for exercises (e.g., "shoulder")
3. Click **+ Add** on desired exercises
4. Set parameters:
   - Sets: 3
   - Reps: 10
   - Frequency: 3x/week
5. Add instructions/modifications
6. Click **Send to Patient**
7. Patient receives SMS with link
8. Also emailed to patient

### Completing Appointment
1. Ensure SOAP note is complete
2. Click **Complete Appointment**
3. Status → "Completed"
4. Front desk notified to process payment
5. You're ready for next patient

## QUICK START FOR OPERATIONS MANAGER

### Daily Dashboard
1. Log in to AIM OS
2. Dashboard shows:
   - Today's appointments
   - Revenue (real-time)
   - Patient volume
   - No-show rate
   - Staff utilization
   - Alerts/issues

### Running Reports

**Daily Revenue Report:**
1. Click **Reports** → **Financial**
2. Select **Daily Revenue**
3. Choose date
4. See:
   - Total billed
   - Total collected
   - Outstanding balances
   - Payment methods breakdown
   - Top services

**Weekly Analytics:**
1. Click **Analytics** → **Weekly Summary**
2. See:
   - Patient volume trends
   - Revenue trends
   - Provider utilization
   - Busiest time slots
   - Referral sources

**Monthly Performance:**
1. Click **Reports** → **Monthly**
2. Export to Excel if needed
3. Review:
   - Total revenue
   - New vs returning patients
   - Service mix
   - Retail sales
   - Insurance claims status

### Managing Reviews
1. Click **Growth** → **Reviews**
2. See:
   - Recent Google reviews
   - Average rating
   - Reviews needing response
3. To respond:
   - Click on review
   - Type response
   - Click **Post Reply**
   - Syncs to Google automatically

### Managing Referrals
1. Click **Growth** → **Referrals**
2. See all referral partners (trainers)
3. Track conversions:
   - Referrals received
   - Appointments booked
   - Conversion rate
4. Send thank-you notes to high performers

## COMMON TASKS

### Rescheduling an Appointment
1. Find appointment in Scheduler
2. Click **Reschedule**
3. Select new date/time
4. Click **Confirm**
5. Patient receives SMS notification

### Canceling an Appointment
1. Find appointment
2. Click **Cancel**
3. Select reason:
   - Patient requested
   - No-show
   - Clinic initiated
   - Other
4. Click **Confirm Cancellation**
5. Patient receives SMS notification

### Handling Direct Billing (Insurance)
1. Create invoice as normal
2. Click **Submit Insurance Claim**
3. Enter:
   - Insurance carrier (e.g., Blue Cross)
   - Policy number
   - Service date
4. Click **Submit Electronically**
5. Claim status tracked in system
6. Patient pays any co-pay/deductible

### Processing WCB Claim
1. Create invoice
2. Click **WCB Claim**
3. Enter:
   - WCB claim number
   - Injury date
   - Employer name
4. Click **Submit**
5. Tracked separately from regular insurance

### Sending Appointment Reminders
**Automatic:** System sends 24-hour reminders automatically

**Manual:**
1. Click appointment
2. Click **Send Reminder**
3. Choose: SMS or Email
4. Click **Send**

### Looking Up Patient History
1. Click **Patients**
2. Search patient name
3. Click on patient
4. See:
   - All past appointments
   - SOAP notes
   - Exercise programs
   - Invoices/payments
   - Insurance claims
   - Communication history

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### "Patient not found"
→ Check phone number or create new patient

### "Provider conflict"
→ That clinician already has appointment at that time
→ Choose different time or provider

### "Payment failed"
→ Check card terminal connection
→ Try swiping again
→ Use backup payment method

### "System is slow"
→ Refresh browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+R)
→ Clear cache
→ Call tech support: (780) 555-TECH

### "Can't log in"
→ Check email address is correct
→ Try "Forgot Password"
→ Contact manager for password reset
